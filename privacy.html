Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Berlusar, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Discord Data: When you authenticate via Discord, we receive your Discord ID, username, and avatar to verify your identity.
  • Log Data: We collect IP addresses and browser fingerprints to prevent abuse and protect our systems.
  • Database Records: Information you provide during registration (username, hashed password) is stored securely in our database.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide authentication and access key services.
  • Protect our platform from bots and malicious users.
  • Ensure the security of your account.

3. Data Protection

Your passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing (Bcrypt). We do not store plain-text passwords. We use secure AES-256 encryption for communication between our client and server.

4. Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our official Discord server.
